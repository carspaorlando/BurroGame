About the Game

Place one card in the middle and wait until your oponent does the same thing

Each time a card is placed in the middle compare it with the target value

if the card matches this value grab as many cards as you can before your oponent does

Whoever is out of cards looses the game.

General Controls

D: to deal the game.

R: to Reset the game.

Player 1 Controls

W: Player 1 plays a card in center

S: hold to grab as many cards as possible.

Player 2 Controls

O: Player 2 place a card in center

L: hold to grab as many cards as possible.
